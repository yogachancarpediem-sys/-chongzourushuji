# Revisiting the Journey into Shu

An interactive web experience blending classical Chinese poetry with ink-wash painting aesthetics, guiding you to retrace Du Fu’s journey into Shu.

## Project Introduction

"Revisiting the Journey into Shu" is a web-based interactive narrative application inspired by Du Fu’s poems from his journey into Shu, combined with ink-wash landscape visuals to immerse users in a poetic voyage across a thousand years of time and space.

Players will follow the footsteps of the Tang Dynasty poet Du Fu, visiting eight scenic waystations along the route, collecting poetic fragments, completing poetry quizzes, unlocking achievements, and ultimately completing this poetic journey.

## Features

- **Ink-Wash Intro Animation**: Rendered using WebGL shaders to create authentic ink-wash landscape effects  
- **Poetic Journey**: Eight thematic waystations showcasing the scenery along Du Fu’s route  
- **Character Interaction**: Two charming protagonists—Liu Xiao Liu and Lin Lu—join you on your journey  
- **Poetry Collection**: Gather poetic fragments along the way  
- **Poetry Quizzes**: Test your knowledge of Du Fu’s poems  
- **Achievement System**: Unlock various achievements to commemorate milestones  
- **Background Music**: Toggleable ambient music feature  
- **Progress Saving**: Automatic game progress saving  

## Getting Started

Simply open `index.html` in your browser to begin the experience.

### Interface Overview

- **Intro Screen**: Ink-wash landscape animation; click “Start Journey” to proceed  
- **Map View**: Explore the locations of all waystations and your current progress  
- **Waystation Details**: Click a waystation to view poems and accompanying stories  
- **Poetry Quizzes**: Challenge your knowledge of Du Fu’s poetry  
- **Poetry Collection**: Review all collected poems  
- **Achievements**: View unlocked achievements  
- **Ending**: View the conclusion after completing the entire journey  

## Technology Stack

- HTML5  
- CSS3 (animations, shader effects)  
- JavaScript (ES6+)  
- WebGL (ink-wash background rendering)

# 重走入蜀记

一个融合中国古典诗词与水墨画风的 interactive web 互动作品，带你重走杜甫入蜀之路。

## 项目介绍

「重走入蜀记」是一款基于 Web 的互动叙事应用，以杜甫入蜀诗作为背景，融合水墨山水画视觉效果，带用户体验一场跨越千年时空的诗旅。

玩家将跟随唐代诗人杜甫的足迹，遍历沿途的各个驿站，收集诗词碎片，完成诗词quiz，解锁成就，最终完成这段诗意旅程。

## 功能特性

- **水墨开场动画**: 使用 WebGL Shader 渲染的水墨山水效果
- **诗词之旅**: 8个主题驿站，带你领略杜甫入蜀沿途风光
- **角色互动**: 两个可爱的主角 - 刘小流和林鲁，与你一同旅行
- **诗词收集**: 收集沿途的诗词碎片
- **诗词问答**: 测试你对杜甫诗词的了解
- **成就系统**: 解锁各种成就纪念
- **背景音乐**: 带有开关的背景音乐功能
- **进度保存**: 自动保存游戏进度

## 开始使用

直接在浏览器中打开 `index.html` 即可开始体验。

### 界面说明

- **开场画面**: 水墨山水动画，点击「开始旅程」进入
- **地图视图**: 查看各驿站位置和当前进度
- **驿站详情**: 点击驿站查看诗词和故事
- **诗词问答**: 测试诗词知识
- **诗词集**: 查看已收集的诗词
- **成就**: 查看已解锁成就
- **结局**: 完成全部旅程后查看

## 技术栈

- HTML5
- CSS3 (动画、Shader 效果)
- JavaScript (ES6+)
- WebGL (水墨背景渲染)
